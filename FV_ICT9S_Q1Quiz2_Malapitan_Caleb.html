Toy story 3

During the events of Toy story 3 Andy is young adult leaving for college, Woody, Buzzlightyear and the other toys get accidentally donated to a day-care center. Unfortunately for them the kids in the day-care center are brutal. After experiencing the brutality of the kids and uncovering a dark secret among the other toys, Woody and the gang plan their escape.
